Inspiration

For many years now I have traveled around the globe, and each time I am affirmed again and again in the incredible potential I see in individuals, even in the most impoverished and oppressed circumstances. Their talents and the way they make the best of what resources they have — no matter how minimal — are what inspires me.

Unfortunately, in spite of great advances in our global society, much of the world population remains so poor that they are not able to develop their equally God-given talents as those of us living in wealthier nations.

I believe that by equipping the less fortunate with resources, training, and support, we can allow them to express their own creative potential as well as assist in their daily sustenance. Ultimately, we need to help them discover and believe in the precious creative gifts that God has uniquely bestowed on all his children — no matter their socioeconomic status. Through developing individual creativity, we in turn help the poor find a means of economic independence and self-dignity. Finally, with confidence and faith, they can then help others discover their gifts as well.
